About the Event

One of the most important and celebrated festival of our Bengali Culture is Durgotsav, in which we celebrate the Victory of Maa Durga over Mahishasur during Navratri, which signifies the triumph of Good over Evil. And for this special and auspicious occasion, we —the members of Sarbojanin Durga Pooja & Bangiya Cultural Association— invite you to our 6th Annual Celebration of Shri Shri Durga Puja from 19th October - 24th October 2023, where great attractions for everyone as under :- 1) Dandiya Night for everybody 2) Cultural Programs with traditional, classical as well as modern touch to it 3) Quiz Shows for kids and adults 4) Fun Games for children of varied age groups 5) Corporate Promotion
